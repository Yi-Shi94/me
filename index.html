Bio

My research interests lie in deep learning, generative models, and computer graphics. I aim to enhance the understanding of human motions and integrate AI into the 3D animation production pipeline. My recent work has concentrated on both kinematic and physically-based motion generation. In the past, I have also worked on NLP and 3D vision.

I'm a PhD student at Simon Fraser University, advised by Professor Xue Bin Peng. Before joining SFU, I worked at Shanghai AI Lab with Jingbo Wang, and XMOV with Congyi Wang. I received my M.Sc. from NYU in 2020 under the supervision of Professor Yi Fang. I received Bachelor's degrees from Tongji University and Politecnico di Torino in 2017.
